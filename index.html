AHC HRSN-Informed Intake

What the self-referral asks

The intake asks for basic contact details, contact preference, and brief health-related social needs questions about food, housing, and transportation. You can choose "Prefer not to answer" for any screening question.

  1. In the past 12 months, did you worry that your food would run out before you had money to buy more?
  2. Do you have housing that is stable and safe?
  3. Do you have difficulty getting transportation to appointments or services?
